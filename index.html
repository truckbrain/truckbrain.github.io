About

TruckBrain is a pioneering system designed to advance driverless trucking through a combination of cutting-edge technologies. It comprises five key innovations: dynamic multi-sensor calibration, allowing adaptable sensor configurations to match truck and trailer sizes; a reliable high-speed wireless network for seamless data transmission between sensors and the central control box; accelerated machine vision algorithms for low-latency data processing while maintaining accuracy; energy-efficient motor control based on traffic perception; and low-latency remote control and monitoring using semantic communications for effective remote operation in scenarios where autonomous driving may not suffice. These innovations collectively contribute to safer and more efficient autonomous trucking operations.TruckBrain's innovative approach not only enhances safety and efficiency but also offers scalability, enabling the addition of redundant sensors for heightened reliability during long-haul transportation. This comprehensive suite of technologies positions TruckBrain as a pivotal player in the evolution of autonomous trucking, bridging the gap between automation and remote operation to ensure seamless and secure logistics solutions.

Technology

Sensor Fusion

Sensor fusion combines data from various sensors like cameras and Lidar to create a comprehensive understanding of the truck's environment.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is the backbone of the project, powering the automation and decision-making capabilities of autonomous trucks, ensuring they navigate safely and efficiently.

Wireless Communication

High-speed wireless communication plays a crucial role in enabling sensor data to be seamlessly transmitted to the central control system, ensuring rapid response and reliable operation of the autonomous truck.

Edge Computing

Edge computing is utilized to reduce data processing latency, enabling swift decision-making and improving the safety and responsiveness of the autonomous driving system.

Open Source Vehicle

CARLA provides a versatile platform for realistic testing and development of autonomous driving algorithms, allowing researchers and developers to fine-tune and validate their systems in a controlled virtual environment.

Artificial Intelligence

AI algorithms are at the core of the project, orchestrating everything from perception to decision-making, resulting in efficient and intelligent autonomous truck operation.
